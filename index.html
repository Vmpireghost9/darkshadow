Code of Conduct

Realm Rules

Break these rules and face the consequences. Respect them and everyone thrives together.

⚔ Combat & Gameplay
01

No Hacking, Cheating or Exploiting

X-ray, autoclickers, duplication glitches, or any exploit giving an unfair advantage is strictly forbidden. Instant permanent ban.

Ban
02

No Combat Logging

Leaving the game during PvP or to avoid dying is not allowed. Stay and fight like a real player.

Ban
03

No Griefing Spawn/Hub

The spawn and hub areas are protected. Griefing these zones is forbidden. Player bases in the wild are fair game for raiding.

Ban
04

PvP is Allowed Everywhere

Fight smart, team up, or risk it all. PvP is enabled across the entire map outside of protected spawn. You were warned.

Allowed
05

Respect Lifesteal Mechanics

Lost a heart fairly in PvP? That's the game. Don't complain to staff about it — it's literally the whole point of the server.

Core Rule
💬 Player Behaviour
06

No Targeted Harassment

Don't constantly bully or single out one player. Light trash talk is fine — relentless targeted harassment is not.

Ban
07

No Extreme Toxicity

Keep it fun. Light banter is fine. Hate speech, racism, or genuinely harmful toxicity will result in a mute or ban.

Ban
08

No Inappropriate Builds, Skins or Names

Keep content clean and appropriate for all ages. Offensive builds, skins, or inappropriate usernames will result in action.

Warning
09

Raiding & Stealing Are Allowed

Raiding bases and stealing items in the wild is part of Lifesteal SMP. Excessively griefing or destroying a base beyond raiding is not permitted.

Allowed
⚙ Server Performance
10

Mining & Grinding Are Allowed

No limits on farms unless they cause lag. If a farm starts affecting server performance, staff may ask you to modify it.

Allowed
11

No Lag Machines or Redstone Spam

Lag machines, excessive TNT, or large redstone contraptions that hurt server performance are forbidden. Excessive chunk loading is also not allowed.

Ban
12

Max CPS is 15

Clicks Per Second are capped at 15. 16–20 CPS may result in a warning or kick. Sustained 20+ CPS counts as cheating and may result in a ban.

Ban
🛡 Miscellaneous
13

No Spawn Killing, Trapping or Portal Trapping

Killing or trapping players the moment they join or respawn is forbidden. Portal trapping in the Nether or End is also not allowed.

Ban
14

Don't Beg for OP, Items or Creative Mode

Don't ask staff for OP, items, creative mode, or any unfair advantage. Everyone plays on equal footing.

Warning
15

Admins' Decisions Are Final

Respect all staff at all times. If you disagree with a decision, appeal through the right channels — don't argue in public chat.

Required
